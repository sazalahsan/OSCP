# OSCP Study Plan (sample)

Week 1: Recon & Enumeration
- Nmap, enum4linux, smbclient, Nikto, gobuster
- Practice: 5 easy HTB boxes focusing on enum

Week 2: Web applications
- Burp, XSS, SQLi, LFI, RFI, file upload
- Practice: DVWA, Juice Shop, Web challenges

Week 3: Exploitation basics
- Buffer overflows (simple), use of gcc/gdb/pattern_create
- Practice: basic buffer overflow labs

Week 4: Privilege escalation
- Linux: SUID files, cron jobs, misconfigs
- Windows: Unquoted paths, weak service permissions

Ongoing:
- Daily 1-2 hours of lab boxes
- Weekly writeups and review
- Keep cheatsheets updated

Notes:
- Adjust pace depending on strengths/weaknesses. Try to write at least 3 full writeups per week.
